Footers

Footers are the last thing on any page of the site. They typically contain site links, social media links, and legal disclaimers and copyrights. Footers can technically be any element, but they are most often used for the footer element of a page. To make a footer, simply add the #footer id to any element.

Currently, the children elements of #footer must all be ul elements. The following classnames should be applied to each of the three ul as well. .nav-links, .social-links, .legal-links
